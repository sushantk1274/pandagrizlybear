About Eco Score

Our comprehensive website meticulously assesses products, utilizing a sophisticated scoring system across four crucial carbon emission dimensions: manufacturing, transportation, usage, and disposal. This holistic approach empowers consumers to make well-informed, eco-conscious decisions, providing a nuanced understanding of a product's environmental impact. By assigning scores in these critical areas, users can easily discern and prioritize eco-friendly options, fostering a sustainable lifestyle and reducing overall carbon footprints.

Green coins distribution

In our commitment to rewarding sustainable choices, users are incentivized through the Green Coins program. By uploading payment receipts for eco-friendly purchases, users accrue Green Coins, which can be redeemed for exclusive gift vouchers. This innovative initiative not only encourages environmentally responsible behavior but also creates a dynamic ecosystem where users actively contribute to a greener planet while enjoying tangible benefits. The Green Coins program promotes a sense of community and shared responsibility.
